Common questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you want to know about working with MAXX.

What is the AI decoration preview?
We use AI tools to build a realistic visual of your exact decoration — your hall, your colours, your flowers, your stage — and send it to your WhatsApp before the event day. You can see exactly how your venue will look and approve every detail before a single flower is placed. This is a MAXX exclusive service — no other event planner in South Tamil Nadu offers this.
How far in advance should I book?
For weddings — minimum 2 months in advance. For birthdays and smaller events — 2 to 4 weeks is sufficient. For peak season (November to February and May to June) — book as early as possible as dates fill quickly. Contact us to check availability for your date.
Do you serve cities outside Madurai?
Yes. We currently serve Madurai, Tirunelveli, Thoothukudi, Nagercoil, Dindigul and Virudhunagar. We are expanding across all of South Tamil Nadu. For NRI families abroad — we coordinate the full event in India while you approve everything remotely. Contact us for your specific location.
What is included in photography?
All packages include candid photography by our founder Vijay Arther — a professional photographer with his own camera equipment. Gold and Diamond packages also include cinematic videography, drone aerial shots, and a highlight reel delivered to WhatsApp within 24 hours of the event. Digital copies of all photos are provided.
Are there any hidden charges?
Never. Every quotation we provide is itemised — you see exactly what each element costs. We do not add charges on the event day. The price you approve in writing is the price you pay. GST invoice provided for all bookings.
How does the MAXX Partner Programme work?
Anyone can join our Partner Programme — vendors, community members, or anyone with a large network. When you refer a family who books with us, you earn 3% of the package fee. Paid to your GPay within 48 hours of advance received. No investment. No targets. DM us "PARTNER" on Instagram to join.
What is the advance amount to confirm a booking?
We require 40% of the total package fee as advance to confirm your booking and date. The remaining 60% is due on or before the event day. We accept GPay, PhonePe, bank transfer, and cash.
